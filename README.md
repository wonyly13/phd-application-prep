# PhD Application Prep 📚

This repository contains all materials and notes for my PhD applications, including research proposals, literature reviews, and progress tracking.

## 📁 Repository Structure
- `cv/`: Different versions of my CV (adapted for different programs).
- `research-proposal/`: Drafts and revisions of my research proposal.
- `target-faculty/`: Notes on potential advisors, including their research papers and my reflections.
- `application-materials/`: Personal statements, recommendation letter drafts, and other documents.
- `literature-notes/`: Key readings and notes related to my research interests (political science, public opinion, urban governance).
